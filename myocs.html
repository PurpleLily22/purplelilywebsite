Atohi

ABOUT
Atohi is the healer of his tribe but he also takes care of the little ones during his free time.
He's a Native American and tiger hybrid.
His favorite object has to be his satchel, where he keeps all the useful herbs.
Yá’át’ééh!
Pronouns: he/him
Height: 195cm (6'4.8''ft)
Favorite food: Navajo tacos with chiles, cheese, onion, lettuce, and beans
Important features:
✦ Tattoos on his face and on his left arm
✦ Hair striped like a tiger's mane
✦ Eyes are also yellow eyes like a tiger’s
FUN FACTS
Atohi is a name of Cherokee Native American origin. It means woods or trees.
According to Cherokee beliefs, trees are known as “the standing people” and symbolize longevity. Their strong roots provide a solid foundation and their constant growth will encourage the child to keep going, no matter how the world around them changes.
